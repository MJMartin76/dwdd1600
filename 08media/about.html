About SaveurQuest

Where authentic flavors meet outdoor adventure

Our Story

SaveurQuest was born from a passion for discovering authentic flavors and hidden outdoor gems around the world. Founded by Mindy Martin as a division of Flavors on the Move LLC, we specialize in taking travelers beyond typical tourist experiences to find genuine cultural connections through food and nature.

Every journey begins with curiosity—a desire to taste something new, explore an unfamiliar trail, or connect with people whose stories enrich our own. SaveurQuest transforms that curiosity into carefully crafted adventures that balance structure with spontaneity, comfort with authenticity, and culinary discovery with outdoor exploration.

Our Mission

We believe authentic travel happens when you step away from crowded tourist spots and engage directly with local communities. Our carefully curated tours focus on:

  • Small group experiences with personalized attention and flexibility
  • Direct partnerships with local food artisans and outdoor guides
  • Sustainable tourism that benefits local communities
  • Educational experiences that deepen cultural understanding
  • Safety and comfort without sacrificing authenticity

What Sets Us Apart

SaveurQuest isn't just another tour company. We're adventurers, food lovers, and culture enthusiasts who have spent years exploring the places we'll take you. Our approach combines:

  • Decades of personal travel experience in our destinations
  • Established relationships with local guides and food artisans
  • A commitment to small groups (typically 8-12 travelers)
  • Itineraries that balance planned activities with free exploration time
  • Focus on experiences that can't be replicated at home

Our Promise

Every tour is carefully planned with your safety and satisfaction in mind. We include backup options for weather variations and believe in delivering exceptional value through genuine cultural immersion and outdoor adventure. We're not just showing you destinations—we're sharing places and experiences we love with people who will appreciate them as much as we do.
