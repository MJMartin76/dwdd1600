About

SaveurQuest Story

SaveurQuest was born from a passion for discovering authentic flavors and hidden outdoor gems around the world. Founded by Mindy Martin as a division of Flavors on the Move LLC, our new venture specializes in taking travelers beyond typical tourist experiences to find genuine cultural connections through food and nature.

Based in the beautiful mountain town of Oakley, Utah, we understand the value of combining spectacular natural landscapes with exceptional culinary discoveries. Our location in Summit County provides the perfect launching point for both local Utah adventures and international expeditions.

SaveurQuest Mission

We believe that authentic travel happens when you step away from crowded tourist spots and engage directly with local communities. Our carefully curated tours focus on:

  • Small group experiences with personalized attention
  • Direct partnerships with local food artisans and outdoor guides
  • Sustainable tourism practices that benefit local communities
  • Educational components that deepen cultural understanding
