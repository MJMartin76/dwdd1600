About SaveurQuest

Where authentic flavors meet outdoor adventure

Our Story

SaveurQuest was born from a passion for discovering authentic flavors and hidden outdoor gems around the world. Founded by Mindy Martin as a division of Flavors on the Move LLC, we specialize in taking travelers beyond typical tourist experiences to find genuine cultural connections through food and nature.

Based in the beautiful mountain town of Oakley, Utah, we understand the value of combining spectacular natural landscapes with exceptional culinary discoveries. Our Summit County location provides the perfect launching point for both local adventures and international expeditions.

Our Mission

We believe authentic travel happens when you step away from crowded tourist spots and engage directly with local communities. Our carefully curated tours focus on:

  • Small group experiences with personalized attention
  • Direct partnerships with local food artisans and outdoor guides
  • Sustainable tourism that benefits local communities
  • Educational experiences that deepen cultural understanding
